About Us

Interlude is a pioneering music company dedicated to transforming the way individuals discover and enjoy music. With an intuitive recommendation layout, Interlude specializes in covering aspects of the music industry that informs music lovers about their favourite or soon to be favourite artists, songs, and albums. Whether you're in the mood to learn about your favourite music genre or seeking to uncover hidden gems in the music world, Interlude has you covered.
